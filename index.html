About Us!



Welcome to Piyush Medcare!


Piyush Medcare is a proud member of Austin Health Partners, an independent Pediatric and Family Medicine Supergroup founded by board-certified physicians.

We provide care for all ages in all over india and look forward to providing you and your family with quality care in your neighborhood.

Our Mission

The mission at Piyush Medcare is to provide patients with the best, most comprehensive care for all ages from birth to seniors. Our practice is committed to giving every patient the best-personalized care.

We accomplish this through close collaboration between our staff, our patients, and their families. Our physicians and nurses are recognized for their compassion, knowledge, and unyielding commitment to excellence.

Our Vision

Everyone should have the potential to become healthy and productive members of their community, and here at Georgetown Pediatrics & Family Medicine, we aspire to be your trusted medical home and safe haven for your family’s health, growth, and development. We strive to give each patient the individual attention they deserve and treat our pediatric patients with care and gentleness as if they were our own. Just consider us part of your family!
